Connect Your TikTok Account

Authorize Kosho Auto Post to publish videos on your behalf via TikTok's official Content Posting API using secure OAuth 2.0.

Authorization Steps

1
Click Connect with TikTok to open TikTok's official authorization page.
2
Sign in to TikTok (if not already) and review the requested permissions.
3
Approve the scopes. You'll be redirected back with your account linked.
4
The Make.com workflow activates automatically and begins posting on schedule.
Permissions Requested
user.info.basic video.publish video.upload
Only these three scopes are requested. No access to DMs, followers, or payment information. Access can be revoked anytime from TikTok Settings → Privacy → Manage app permissions.

What Kosho Auto Post Does
🔐

Secure OAuth 2.0

Uses TikTok's official authorization flow. Your credentials are never stored — only a scoped access token that can be revoked at any time.

⚡

Automated Publishing

Videos publish automatically on schedule via Make.com. No manual uploads — the workflow handles everything from creation to posting.

📊

Content Dashboard

Track every published, scheduled, and draft post from a single dashboard. Monitor view counts and manage your entire content calendar.

🎦

AI Video Creation

Integrates with Creatomate to generate dynamic AI-powered videos. From concept to published TikTok — fully automated.

📅

Advanced Scheduling

Queue posts days in advance. The system handles timing automatically, publishing at optimal hours even while you're offline.

🔒

Revocable Access

Users can revoke authorization at any time from TikTok Settings. No data is retained after disconnection.

API Products & Permission Scopes

user.info.basic

Reads the authenticated user's display name and avatar to show in the dashboard. No private data accessed.

video.upload

Uploads video files to TikTok's servers in preparation for publishing. Used as the first step of the posting workflow.

video.publish

Publishes uploaded videos to TikTok. Only triggered by the automated workflow with user-approved content.
